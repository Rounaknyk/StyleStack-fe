StyleStack: Your Style AI

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 July 2026
Last updated: 20 July 2026

StyleStack: Your Style AI (“StyleStack,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a personal wardrobe and styling application. This policy explains what information we collect, why we use it, when it may be shared, how long it is kept, and the choices available to you. By using StyleStack, you acknowledge these practices.

1. Information we collect

Account and identity

We may collect your Firebase user identifier, name, email address, profile photo, phone number, authentication/session information, and account timestamps, depending on how you sign in. Firebase Authentication and any sign-in provider you choose also process information under their own policies.

Profile and personalization

We may collect your display name, date of birth, gender identity or “prefer not to say” selection, height, body-type selection, style preferences, shopping frequency, styling goals, city, time zone, notification settings, favorites, selections, and feedback. Body-related information is used only for styling and proportion guidance, not medical assessment.

Wardrobe, images, and styles

We collect wardrobe photos you take, select, or share; item names, brands, categories, colors, sizes, seasons, formality, tags, descriptions, notes, favorites, purchase information; AI labels and visual tags; image fingerprints, thumbnails, and background-removed cutouts; saved outfits and their reasoning; canvas arrangements and previews; wear logs; and any outfit-history images already submitted through a supported feature. Private images are made available to the app through time-limited access links.

Location

If you grant permission, StyleStack may use your precise location while the app is open to determine your city. Coordinates are used transiently for this purpose; the resulting city and time zone may be stored for weather-aware recommendations. You can deny or revoke permission and continue using StyleStack.

Optional Gmail Closet Sync

After you connect Google and grant the requested Gmail access, StyleStack may search qualifying ecommerce delivery messages and read the sender, subject, order identifier, relevant product text, and product-image references needed to create wardrobe items. We do not ask for or store your Gmail password. Email HTML and temporary image bytes are not intentionally retained after import. Product details, images, source, and an external order identifier may be saved to create items and prevent duplicates. Unrelated, promotional, and non-qualifying messages are intended to be ignored.

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Optional Google Calendar

If connected, we may store your Google account email, an authorization credential for background synchronization, event identifiers, titles, descriptions, locations, start/end times, all-day status, last-sync time, and linked StyleStack outfits. This supports event-aware suggestions and reminders. Disconnecting stops future access, removes the saved credential, and removes imported Google events from StyleStack.

Notifications, device data, and local storage

If enabled, we store a Firebase Cloud Messaging token and platform to deliver outfit alerts, event reminders, and processing updates. We may store notification content, read status, deduplication identifiers, and timestamps. We also process limited diagnostics such as request time, response status, app version, and platform. We do not intentionally log passwords, authentication tokens, service keys, email bodies, or uploaded-image contents.

The app uses device preferences and a local database to cache wardrobe information, remember settings and disclosures, speed up loading, and provide limited offline access. Local data may remain until you sign out, clear app data, delete your account, or uninstall the app.

2. How we use information

  • Create, authenticate, maintain, and secure your account.
  • Store, organize, search, and display your wardrobe.
  • Remove image backgrounds, optimize images, and generate tags.
  • Create personalized daily, event-aware, and conversational outfit recommendations.
  • Use wear history, favorites, and choices to improve future suggestions.
  • Import eligible purchases and sync authorized calendar events.
  • Provide city-based weather guidance, notifications, saved styles, and sharing at your direction.
  • Detect duplicates, prevent abuse, manage limits, troubleshoot, respond to requests, and comply with law.

3. Artificial intelligence

StyleStack uses automated systems for image analysis, clothing metadata, outfit combinations, and explanations. Depending on configuration, necessary image content, wardrobe metadata, profile preferences, event context, city/weather context, or stylist-chat messages may be sent to AI providers such as Groq or Google Gemini. AI output can be inaccurate; you can review and edit item details and decide whether to follow recommendations. AI output is not used for medical, employment, credit, insurance, legal, or other high-impact decisions.

4. Sharing and service providers

We do not sell personal information and do not currently use it for third-party behavioral advertising or cross-app tracking.

We disclose only what is needed to providers that operate StyleStack, including Google Firebase (authentication and messaging), Supabase (database and private storage), backend hosts such as Render, Groq and Google Gemini (AI processing), OpenWeather (city-based weather), Google APIs (optional Gmail/Calendar), and Pexels (optional style inspiration). Their own policies apply.

If you use the system share sheet, the content you select is provided to the recipient or app you choose. We may also preserve or disclose information to comply with law, protect safety and security, investigate abuse, enforce rights, or complete a merger, financing, reorganization, or asset transfer subject to applicable law.

5. Legal bases

Where required, we process information to perform our contract and provide requested features; with consent for optional permissions, integrations, and notifications; for legitimate security, reliability, fraud-prevention, and improvement interests; and to comply with legal obligations. You may withdraw consent for optional processing at any time.

6. Retention

We generally keep account and user-created content while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Temporary import content is kept for a shorter period. When you delete content, disconnect an integration, or delete your account, associated active records and owned storage are deleted. Limited information may remain temporarily in routine backups, security records, or provider logs until rotation. We may retain information longer when legally required or necessary for fraud prevention, disputes, or enforcement.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, consent withdrawal, or lodge a complaint with a regulator. You can edit many fields in the app, revoke device permissions in system settings, disable notifications, and disconnect Google access. Contact rondevelops1904@gmail.com. We may verify your identity before completing a request.

8. Account and data deletion

Delete your account in the app at Profile → Account → Delete account. After confirmation, StyleStack attempts to revoke connected Google Calendar access, delete private storage files, delete your profile and associated wardrobe items, outfits, wear history, calendar events, notifications, device tokens and saved styles, delete the Firebase Authentication account, and sign you out.

You may also email rondevelops1904@gmail.com from the account email with the subject “StyleStack account deletion.” Do not send passwords or authentication codes. Verification may be required. Deletion is permanent. Data legally required for security, fraud prevention, disputes, or compliance may be retained only as necessary. See the dedicated account deletion instructions.

9. Security

We use reasonable safeguards including authenticated APIs, user-scoped queries, private storage, restricted server credentials, and transport encryption where supported. No system is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. International transfers

StyleStack and its providers may process information outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards or lawful transfer mechanisms.

11. Children

StyleStack is not directed to children under 13. Users must satisfy the minimum age for online consent in their country or use the service only with parent or guardian authorization where permitted. Contact us if you believe a child submitted information without appropriate authorization.
