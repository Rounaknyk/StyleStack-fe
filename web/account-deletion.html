Delete your StyleStack account

These instructions apply to StyleStack: Your Style AI. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete inside the app

Profile → Account → Delete account

  1. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Open Profile and scroll to Account.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Type DELETE and confirm permanent deletion.

Request deletion without app access

Email rondevelops1904@gmail.com from the email associated with your account. Use the subject StyleStack account deletion. Do not send a password, one-time code, or authentication token. We may request reasonable verification before acting.

What is deleted

StyleStack attempts to delete your authentication account, profile and personalization data, wardrobe records and private images, saved outfits and canvas styles, wear history, imported calendar events and stored Calendar authorization, notifications, and registered device tokens.

What may be retained

Limited information may remain temporarily in routine backups, security records, or service-provider logs until rotation. Information required for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution may be retained only for the necessary period.
