# Privacy Policy for StyleStack: Your Style AI

**Effective date:** 20 July 2026  
**Last updated:** 21 July 2026

StyleStack: Your Style AI (“StyleStack,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a personal
wardrobe and styling application. This Privacy Policy explains what information
we collect, why we use it, when it may be shared, how long it is kept, and the
choices available to you.

By using StyleStack, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy
Policy. If you do not agree with them, please do not use the service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account and identity information

When you create or use an account, we may collect:

- your Firebase user identifier;
- your name, email address, profile photo, or phone number, depending on the
  sign-in method you choose;
- authentication and session information needed to keep you signed in; and
- account creation and update timestamps.

Authentication is provided through Firebase Authentication. When you use Google
Sign-In or another third-party sign-in method, that provider also processes
information under its own privacy policy.

### 1.2 Personalization and profile information

To personalize styling recommendations, we may collect information you provide
during onboarding or in settings, including:

- display name and date of birth;
- gender identity or a “prefer not to say” selection;
- height and body-type selection;
- style preferences, shopping frequency, and styling goals;
- city, time zone, notification preference, and reminder time; and
- your feedback, selections, favorites, and other preference signals.

We use body-related information only to provide proportion and fit guidance. We
do not use it to make medical assessments or to infer health conditions.

### 1.3 Wardrobe, image, and styling information

When you add or create content, we may collect:

- wardrobe and clothing photos you take, select, or share with StyleStack;
- item names, brands, categories, colors, sizes, seasons, formality, tags,
  descriptions, notes, favorites, purchase dates, prices, and currency;
- AI-generated labels, visual tags, image fingerprints, thumbnails, and
  background-removed cutouts;
- saved outfits, outfit reasoning, canvas arrangements, preview images, and
  style history;
- wear logs, including the items you mark as worn and the date, time, or notes;
  and
- any outfit-history images already submitted through a supported feature.

Your wardrobe images and style previews are stored in private cloud storage and
are made available to the app through time-limited access links.

### 1.4 Location information

If you grant location permission, StyleStack may access your device’s precise
location while the app is in use to determine your city. The coordinates are
used transiently for this purpose; StyleStack stores the resulting city and
time zone for weather-aware recommendations. You may deny or revoke location
permission and continue using the app, although weather-aware recommendations
may be less specific.

### 1.5 Gmail Closet Sync information

Gmail Closet Sync is optional and only runs after you choose to connect a Google
account and grant the requested Gmail permission. When enabled, StyleStack may:

- search eligible ecommerce delivery messages;
- read the sender, subject, order identifier, relevant product text, and product
  image references needed to identify delivered fashion purchases; and
- create wardrobe items from qualifying purchases.

StyleStack does not ask for or store your Gmail password. Email HTML and
temporary downloaded image data are not intentionally retained after the import
process. Product details, product images, the import source, and an external
order identifier may be saved with the imported wardrobe item to prevent
duplicates. Promotional, unrelated, and non-qualifying messages are intended to
be ignored.

Google API information is used only to provide user-facing StyleStack features.
Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will comply with
the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use
requirements.

### 1.6 Google Calendar information

Google Calendar connection is optional. If you connect it, StyleStack may
access and store:

- your connected Google account email;
- an authorization credential that allows background calendar synchronization;
- event identifiers, titles, descriptions, locations, start and end times, and
  all-day status; and
- the last synchronization time and the StyleStack outfit linked to an event.

This information is used to show your events, create event-aware outfit
recommendations, and send reminders you enable. You can disconnect Google
Calendar in the app. Disconnecting revokes future access where available,
removes the stored authorization credential, and removes imported Google
Calendar events from StyleStack.

### 1.7 Notifications and device information

If you enable notifications, we may collect a Firebase Cloud Messaging device
token and device platform. We use these to deliver morning outfit suggestions,
event reminders, processing updates, and test notifications you request. We may
also store in-app notification content, delivery deduplication identifiers,
read status, and timestamps.

You can disable notifications in StyleStack or in your device settings.

### 1.8 Technical information and local storage

StyleStack may process technical information such as request timestamps,
response status, app version, platform, device and app identifiers, screen
views, feature interactions, and limited diagnostic information needed to
understand use, secure the service, and improve reliability. Google Analytics
for Firebase provides product analytics and may automatically collect app
instance, device, operating-system, language, approximate-location, session,
and interaction information as described in Google's documentation. StyleStack
does not send wardrobe photos, item names, stylist-chat text, email contents,
phone numbers, or calendar-event text as custom analytics event parameters.
We do not intentionally log passwords, authentication tokens, service keys,
email bodies, or uploaded image contents.

The app uses device storage, preferences, and a local database to cache wardrobe
information, remember settings and disclosures, support faster loading, and
provide limited offline access. Local data may remain on your device until you
sign out, clear app data, delete your account, or uninstall the app.

### 1.9 Optional rewarded advertising

StyleStack may offer an optional Google AdMob rewarded advertisement after you
use the daily free outfit refreshes or after you connect Google Calendar. You
can decline the offer and keep using the app; connecting Calendar never depends
on watching an advertisement. If you choose to view one, Google Mobile Ads may
process advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location, device and
app information, ad interactions, and diagnostic information to request,
deliver, measure, prevent fraud in, and personalize or limit ads as permitted by
your settings, consent choices, and applicable law. StyleStack records whether
the reward was earned so it can provide the promised bonus outfit refresh.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- create, authenticate, maintain, and secure your account;
- store, organize, search, and display your digital wardrobe;
- remove image backgrounds, create optimized images, and generate item tags;
- generate personalized daily, event-aware, and conversational styling
  recommendations;
- avoid recently worn items and improve suggestions from your selections and
  wear history;
- import eligible purchases and synchronize connected calendar events;
- provide weather-aware comfort guidance using your stored city;
- deliver notifications and in-app messages you request or enable;
- offer optional rewarded advertisements and provide the disclosed in-app
  reward when earned;
- save, edit, display, and share styles at your direction;
- detect duplicate images, prevent abuse, manage service limits, troubleshoot
  failures, and improve reliability;
- respond to support, privacy, and account-deletion requests; and
- comply with applicable law and enforce our rights.

## 3. Artificial Intelligence Processing

StyleStack uses automated systems to analyze wardrobe images and generate
clothing metadata, outfit combinations, and explanations. Depending on
availability and configuration, relevant image content, wardrobe metadata,
profile preferences, event context, city/weather context, or a stylist-chat
message may be sent to AI service providers such as Groq or Google Gemini.

AI output can be incomplete or inaccurate. You can review and edit item details
and choose whether to follow a recommendation. StyleStack does not use AI output
for medical, employment, credit, insurance, legal, or other high-impact
decisions.

## 4. When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. Google AdMob may use advertising and
device information to deliver or measure ads, including personalized ads where
allowed by your consent choices, device settings, and applicable law.

We may disclose information in these circumstances:

### 4.1 Service providers

We use providers that process information for us only as needed to operate
StyleStack, including:

- **Google Firebase** for authentication, push messaging, and app analytics;
- **Google AdMob** for optional rewarded advertising, measurement, and ad-fraud
  prevention;
- **Supabase** for database and private file storage;
- **backend hosting providers** such as Render for application processing;
- **Groq and Google Gemini** for AI-supported image and styling analysis;
- **OpenWeather** for weather information based on city;
- **Google APIs** for optional Gmail and Calendar connections; and
- **Pexels** for optional style-inspiration search results.

These providers process information under their own terms and privacy policies.
App-store privacy declarations include relevant data practices of integrated
third-party software development kits and service providers.

### 4.2 At your direction

If you use a system share sheet to share a style or image to Instagram,
WhatsApp, or another app, the selected recipient or platform receives the
content you choose to share. Their privacy practices then apply.

### 4.3 Legal, safety, and business protection

We may preserve or disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply
with law, legal process, or enforceable government requests; protect users,
StyleStack, or the public; investigate fraud or security incidents; or enforce
our terms and rights.

### 4.4 Business transfer

If StyleStack is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization,
bankruptcy, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that
transaction, subject to applicable law and this Privacy Policy.

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing

Where a legal basis is required, we process information:

- to perform our contract with you and provide the features you request;
- with your consent, including for optional device permissions, Gmail,
  Calendar, and notifications;
- for legitimate interests such as security, reliability, fraud prevention, and
  service improvement, where those interests do not override your rights; and
- to comply with legal obligations.

You may withdraw consent for optional processing at any time. Withdrawal does
not affect processing already completed lawfully.

## 6. Data Retention

We generally keep account and user-created content while your account is active
or as needed to provide StyleStack. Specific information may be kept for a
shorter period when it is temporary, such as email HTML and image bytes used
during Gmail import.

When you delete individual content, disconnect an integration, or delete your
account, StyleStack deletes the associated active records and owned private
storage as described below. Limited information may remain temporarily in
routine backups, security records, or service-provider logs until those records
are rotated or deleted. We may retain information longer when required by law,
needed to resolve disputes, prevent fraud or abuse, or enforce agreements.

## 8. Account and Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account from:

**Profile → Account → Delete account**

After confirmation, StyleStack attempts to:

1. revoke connected Google Calendar access;
2. delete files stored under your private StyleStack storage area;
3. delete your profile and associated wardrobe items, outfits, wear history,
   calendar events, notifications, device tokens, and saved styles; and
4. delete your Firebase Authentication account and sign you out.

You may also request deletion by emailing **rondevelops1904@gmail.com** from the
email associated with your account. Include “StyleStack account deletion” in
the subject. Do not send passwords or authentication codes. We may request
reasonable verification before deletion.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Information that must be
retained for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes
may be kept only for the necessary period.

You are responsible for keeping your device and sign-in credentials secure.
Contact us promptly if you believe your account has been compromised.
